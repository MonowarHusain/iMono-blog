Do you ever feel like you're caught in a loop? A cycle of big dreams, exciting ideas, and genuine ambition, constantly overshadowed by a nagging lack of energy? Breaking free from this cycle isn't about sudden, monumental shifts in willpower. It's about strategic, consistent action. Let's explore the core functions:

### Step One: Make It Small – The Power of Tiny Steps

Big tasks trigger the `procrastination_alarm`. The antidote? Shrink them down. Dramatically.

Imagine your goal is to `writeBook()`. Instead of focusing on 300 pages, redefine your immediate task: 
`String todayOutput = "One sentence.";`

Small steps provide immediate, positive feedback. You're not trying to leap to the mountaintop; you're taking one deliberate step, then another. Remember, tiny droplets of water can carve canyons. 

### Step Two: Create Easy Routines – Automate Success

Willpower is a finite resource. It's like a battery that depletes throughout the day. The solution? **Build routines.**

Routines transform intentional actions into automatic behaviors. For instance, if your goal is to exercise regularly:
- **Input:** Lay out workout clothes next to the bed.
- **Process:** Wake up.
- **Output:** The path of least resistance is to put them on.

The decision has already been made, reducing the friction of starting.
